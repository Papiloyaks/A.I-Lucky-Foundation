ABOUT US

AI Lucky Foundation

an international 501(c)3 tax-exempt charity, concentrates on a bottom-up approach to erasing poverty by meeting basic human needs, while also providing the tools to gain control of one's future.

We Believe In Water Plus Electricity

Our effort is to bring water plus electricity to remote small villages around the world.

To empower fathers and mothers, men and women, boys and girls, with these life transforming resources.

Giving opportunity for a brighter future.

Changing lives one village at a time.

AI Lucky Foundation

Over several months the idea of tranforming lives through access to water plus electricity was shared with individuals from all over the globe.

Invaluable feedback was received from the very people we wish to serve.

The vision was matured, and our effort was realized, with the start of AI Lucky Foundation in late 2019.

But the global pandemic almost shut down our organization before it ever got started.

Yet with perseverence, today AI Lucky Foundation is a registered 501(c)3 tax-exempt charity moving forward with a mission to provide a life-changing foundation to remote small communities around the world.

HUMAN CAPITAL DEVELOPMENT

ABUBAKAR ISAH

AKA ENGR. LUCKY

Abubakar Isah was born in the late part of the 1980s to the family of Isah Sule, military personnel, now retired and his late mother a business woman and they hail from Imane District of Olamaboro Local Government Area of Kogi State. As a son of providence and going by the circumstance of his birth, Abubakar was named by neighbours and those who were aware of how it was almost impossible for the mother and him to make it at delivery as Lucky, the name that has accompanied him till date.

Abubakar Isah (aka Lucky) had his early education at Owelle Islamic Primary School Ankpa, Kogi State where he got his First School Leaving Certificate in the year 2000. He proceeded to the famous St.Charles College Ankpa where he had a brief stint.

As a result of his inquistive nature and local for technology and skills related tasks, Soja-his father felt it was better to enroll the young Abubakar in a technical school, and that landed the brilliant boy in the famous Technical College Ankpa where he wrote his NABTEB examination and came out in flying colours 2006.

As at this time, Abubakar's zeal for Engineering was already up to the high heavens, and despite meagree resources resolved to pursue his lofty dreams and aspirations. As God will have it, he got admission to the ancient Benue State Polytechnic Ugbokolo, Benue State, and Lucky's journey to become an Engineer began. In the year 2009, he bagged his Diploma in Civil Engineering. He is at the verge of concluding a Higher National Diplama, in Civil Engineering. He is at the verge of concluding a Higher National Diploma, HND in his Engineering field to give him better leverage in the future.

While Abubakar was contemplating how to raise the required fund to further his studies, he got an invitation from CCC Abuja, the very company with which he did his Mandatory Industrial Training (IT). This obviously was because he had excelled and lived above board earlier. The company then employed and made him a Foreman in charge of Earthmoving Department.

Barely three years later in 2013 Lucky was elected as the Vice Chairman of the workers union of CCC Nigeria Limited. In 2015, he was promoted to the position of Public Relations Officer (CSO) of the company

Lucky in the meantime, started a Barchelor's degree and in 2021, graduated with a degree in Government and Public Administration from Imo State University As a result of his competency and exceptional qualities, the company appointed him as the Human Resource Manager in 2021, a position he has held till date. Abubarkar Isah is fully Human capital development in the society. May God keep sailing him through all is journey of achievement.
