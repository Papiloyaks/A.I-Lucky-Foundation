Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a meaning for the name AI Lucky Foundation?

Yes, there is. It is actually in three parts. The first part is "Transform", which means to positively affect change. The second part is "Through", which means generous giving and volunteering. The third is "WE", which is an acronym for Water and Electricity and is also the untold number of wonderful people that provide a better life for those in need.

What is the goal of AI Lucky Foundation?

Our goal is to provide access to electricity to a small village (or combination of villages) of 1,000 people around the world. With this access, we provide five critical services; Safe water from water filtration systems, Light at night from lanterns, clean cooking from dual hot plates, medicine storage with refrigeration, and internet access. With electricity and these services, small villages will have a solid foundation to build from.

Why do you say electricity is a game changer?

With access to electricity, we are able to bring a number of efforts together to help those in need. Safe drinkable water, cleaner air to breathe, and internet access for education to start. Serving 1,000 people for a minimum of 10 years that can grow with the needs of the village. And that is not all. Access to electricity will help reduce human trafficking, reverse deforestation, help keep families together, provide opportunities to start small businesses, and dramatically increase the quality of life. To finish, eliminating the need to leave the village daily and having light from sundown to bedtime frees up to 8 hours or more a day, every day. Saving time = Giving time. Think of the possibilities! All of this is possible, due to the fact that people have access to electricity.

Is AI Lucky Foundation affiliated with any political or religious organizations?

No. AI Lucky Foundation does not have any affiliations with any political or religious organizations.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes. Financial and in-kind donations are deductible under applicable laws. AI Lucky Foundation is a U.S. registered 501(c)3 charity. Our tax ID# is 84-2377911.

What percentage of my donation will go directly to the cause?

Currently it is 82%. That is 82 cents of every dollar donated goes directly to the cause. The percentage can go higher when more projects are funded and if the donors choose to include the merchant service fees (5%) with the donations. (ex: $15 donation plus .75 cents for a total of $15.75). That could increase the directed amount to 87% or more.
