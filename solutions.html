OUR SOLUTION

A Solution That Transforms

Our solution eliminates the divide between poverty and prosperity.

For an estimated one billion people around the world, lack of access to water and electricity stands between them and a better life. Transform Through WE offers a solution called the Life Transformation System, a permanent off-grid solution, that breaks down the barriers of daily poverty not just for today or for the near term, but for generations to come.

How It Works

We capture the energy from the sun and wind

With solar panels and wind turbines

The energy is routed to storage batteries

When needed, energy is converted to electricity

Services Provided

Safe Water Provided By Water Filtration

Light At Night Provided By Lanterns

Clean Cooking Provided By Hot Plates

Refrigeration For Medications

Communication Access And Internet

Providing just the first three services above (safe water, light at night, and clean cooking) would be considered life transforming, but with the consistent availability of electricity, we are able to provide refrigeration and communication which greatly extends the opportunities for positive change.

Impact

Immediate

The daily need to search for water and substances for fire is no longer needed

Open fires for light at night and for cooking are no longer needed

Frees up to 8 hours a day, every day Think of the possibilities!

Long Term

Slow, stop or reverse family separation

Reduce the opportunity for human trafficking

Reverse deforestation

Dramatically increase quality of life!

While the Life Transformation System will provide for 1,000 people, the reality is that a remote village will not typically be this size. This means that instead of just one village, we can provide for 5 villages at 200 people each or 10 villages at 100 people each or even possibly 20 villages at 50 people each!
