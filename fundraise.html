Help transform lives!

Your voice, your friends, and your passion can unlock the potential of an entire community!

READY TO START YOUR OWN CAMPAIGN?

It's easy! Send us your contact information below.

We will follow up to answer all your questions, assist in getting your personal fundraising page designed and be available throughout your fundraising time.

Thank you for your desire to help in saving. and transforming lives!

Get Started With Your Fundraiser

Personalize Your Message

Be creative with your message. Tell people why you are so passionate about your effort and why you decided to get involved with Transform Through WE. It doesn't have to be long; just what comes naturally and from the heart! This can be written or you can create a video with your cell phone. A video appeal is very impactful. Make it yours!

Setup Your Campaign Page

Creating your personal campaign page is quick and easy.Simply send us a picture of yourself that you want to use and your written message. Send your video if you created one. Once received your page will be completed in one day and will be ready for you to get started!

Spread The Word

Now the fun begins! Get started by reaching out to friends and family but don't stop there. Think outside the box. Invite coworkers, colleages, neighbors, acquaintances, and even strangers to give. Raising awareness of the problem and your efforts to help solve it will make a huge difference!

Ways To Connect

Once your campaign is set up, tell everyone about it! Use email (we will provide a PDF document telling about your campaign). Send texts to direct people to your campaign page. Post on your social media (Facebook, Instagram, etc.). Print out the PDF document to hand out to people you meet face to face. The more people you tell, the quicker you will reach your goal. Have fun!

Watch As Donations Come In

Your campaign page will update as donations come in. Make sure to ask your donors to post a comment about your campaign. The comment will show up with their donation so others can see. It will help get more people excited when they look at your campaign page.

Celebrate!

When your campaign is completed send out a "Thank You and Well Done!" message to all of your donors. We will do the same. Monthly updates will be sent out to you and your supporters from this point until the Life Transformation System is installed and serving the village.

Frequently Asked Questions

I would love to get involved, but I don't know where to begin.

Need some assistance? Need inspiration? Whatever you need, we are here to help! Sign up below and we will assist you in writing the perfect messages, to help designing your fundraising page, to being available for any questions you might have throughout your campaign, and finally celebrating with you when you meet your goal!

I have my fundraising page finished and my messages ready. Now what?

Great! Now the fun begins! Be the first donor. Make a great comment! It shows that you are all in. Start posting on your social network (Facebook, Instagram, etc.). Send emails and texts. As people start to give, you will see their donations on your fundraising page. It is very important to respond with a thank you message. We will also send a thank you message to your donors as well.

I want to make a bigger impact. How can I do this?

Add a challenge to all of your messages and to your fundraising page. This is a great way to get others involved. See below why and how a challenge works.

Why a challenge?

It is critical that as much of the funds that Transform Through WE receives go directly to the effort. Having passionate, excited supporters sharing with their social network greatly reduces the expenses that Transform Through WE needs to spend to get the word out. The more challenges that are initiated and completed, the more Life Transformation Systems will be installed. The bottom line, more lives will be transformed!

"15 For 15" Challenge

A simple and easy, yet very powerful way for you to fundraise. The challenge is in two parts. The first is for you to give a one-time donation of $15. Simple and easy. The second is for you to share about our effort through your social network (friends, family, coworkers, etc.) and challenge them to do the same as you have done, which is to give a one-time donation of $15 and share it with their social network. That is the powerful part! That is it! When people accept the challenge, we will follow up with them and keep the excitement going! Simple... Easy... Powerful...

Why only A $15 one-time donation?

There are a number of reasons. First, we wanted to make the amount to give an easy decision. Second, with respect to those who choose to help fundraise, we wanted to make the challenge amount easy as well. Third, we don't want to take away any funds from other nonprofits you, and those that you contact, support.

I am a college student. What is the best way to promote AI Lucky Foundation on campus?

First you need to check with your school to make sure that it is ok to raise money on campus. If it is ok, be sure to know the rules and steps to follow. Then start with setting up your fundraising page and get your messages ready. Now is the fun part. You have a number of ways to promote your fundraising challenge on campus. Be as creative as you want. Start a "15 For 15" Challenge and reach out to the students you know. Challenge them to get the word out. Challenge your fraternity or sorority if you are in one. Take the challenge to the next level and challenge other fraternities and sororities. In no time you can be reaching the whole student body and providing a Life Transformation System to a village that desperately needs to be lifted out of poverty.

Lastly, we want to show just how much of an impact a small one-time donation can make. Combined with organic fundraising starting with your social network, to the untold numbers of people that will be touched by your friends, family, and coworkers, and beyond to give is powerful. From the beginning of the project to the onsite installation, everyone's efforts will be realized through a video documentary seeing the lives of people transformed!
