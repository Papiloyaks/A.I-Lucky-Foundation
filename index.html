AI Lucky Foundation,

ELEVATING QUALITY OF LIFE

is our mission

From helping fathers provide for their families, empowering women, and encouraging children, to improving everyone's level of health we believe Water, plus Electricity, (the WE in our name) is the best solution to elevating their quality of life.

Yet, 1 billion people globally have no access.

We are determined to change this.

OBJECTIVES OF THE ABUBAKAR ISAH LUCKY FOUNDATION

1. Assist directly or facilitate the human capital development of indigent Nigerians especially Kogites through manpower skills training.

2. Repositioning the girl child and by extension, women for greater impact in contemporary.

3. Raising global business and professional leaders through value reorientation

4. Facilitate the provision of portable water in rural communities

5. Promotion of education especially among rural dwellers

OUR SOLUTION delivers a life-changing foundation to small remote villages that opens the door to possibilities!
